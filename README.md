Marc Usher - Full-Stack Software Engineer
==================================================================================================================================

I'm a multilingual Software Engineer with professional software engineering experience and over 8 years experience in Digital Marketing at internationally-renowned HE institutions.

I've developed public transport data analytics products at [Ito World](https://www.itoworld.com/) for the Department for Transport and major transport clients as part of a full-stack engineering team, with a focus on Django and Ariadne (GraphQL) API back-end architecture connecting with PostgreSQL databases in Dockerized microservice-structured products. At Ito World I also worked on the [**Bus Open Data Service (BODS)**](https://github.com/department-for-transport-BODS/bods/commits?author=marcusher-ito&since=2023-01-01&until=2023-03-01), a full-stack Django application linking to PostgreSQL databases, where the work ranged from data modelling and data handling to front-end development in HTML and SASS.

I followed my passion for digital innovation and software engineering and retrained with General Assembly's full-time immersive Software Engineering course, which equipped me with a strong foundation in full-stack development and proficiency in a range of technologies, as well as hands-on experience building full-stack applications from the ground up in an Agile development workflow; interpeting project requirements from the original brief, planning out data structures and application architecture, and creating and deploying full-stack applications in the MERN stack and in Django with a PostgreSQL database.

I bring a positive impact to internal teams and practices with my collaboration skills and attention to detail, while my strong stakeholder and project management skills enable successful internal and external relationships.
